# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive and professional open-source community.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing problems clearly.
- Keep discussion focused on improving the project.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality, especially around meeting data.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, abuse or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Introducing code that intentionally weakens security, privacy or approval safeguards.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community and the project.
