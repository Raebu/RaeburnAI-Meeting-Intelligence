# Product specification

## Positioning

RaeburnAI Meeting Intelligence is not a transcription archive. It is a meeting-to-workflow automation layer.

## Primary users

- Consultancies
- SMEs
- Customer success teams
- Sales and account teams
- Product and engineering teams
- Agencies with recurring client meetings

## User stories

1. As a consultant, I want meeting decisions captured automatically so that nothing is lost after a client call.
2. As a delivery lead, I want actions extracted and assigned to owners so that follow-up is immediate.
3. As a sales leader, I want CRM notes and next steps prepared so that pipeline hygiene improves.
4. As a product manager, I want Jira/GitHub tickets drafted so that meeting outcomes become backlog items.
5. As an operator, I want approvals before writeback so that AI cannot silently change business systems.

## Differentiators

- Outcome-first instead of transcript-first.
- Approval-first governance.
- Multi-system writeback.
- Local deterministic mode.
- Open-source and extensible.

## Success metrics

- Percentage of meetings with detected decisions.
- Percentage of meetings with assigned action owners.
- Time from meeting end to approved follow-up.
- Number of CRM updates created.
- Number of Jira/GitHub tasks created.
- Human correction rate.
- Missed-owner rate.

## Non-goals for v1

- Owning the audio transcription layer.
- Replacing enterprise CRM workflow engines.
- Sending unapproved external updates by default.
